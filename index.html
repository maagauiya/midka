Training program

Total duration of the course: 27 academic hours.


№1 Plot and composition

The teacher will tell you how to determine the proportions of the subject “by eye” and place the elements of the composition on the sheet. What is perspective? We try to create a still life from geometric shapes with a pencil.

№2 Black and white balance

You will learn how to use pencil hatching to convey the volume and depth of the image. Types of hatching and tonal transitions. Principles of chiaroscuro, how to make highlights on the depicted objects.

№3 Drawing from nature

What is an aerial perspective and how to use it to make an image light and airy. We draw a still life from nature, Train in the transfer of the size, volume and structure of various objects in the drawing.

№4 Still life in detail

Detailed study of all the elements of the still life. We practice various types of hatching, learn how to make soft transitions and transfer the texture of objects.

№5 Draw landmarks

Detailed study of all the elements of the still life. We practice various types of hatching, learn how to make soft transitions and transfer the texture of objects.

№6 Working with color
